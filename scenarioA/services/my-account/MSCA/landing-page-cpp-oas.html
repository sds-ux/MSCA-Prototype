Take a look at our online Pension tools for Canada Pension Plan (CPP), Old Age Security (OAS), and the Guaranteed Income Supplement (GIS) below.

CPP Applications 


Apply for CPP or view detailed information on your application status.

OAS/GIS Applications 


Apply for OAS/GIS or view detailed information on your application status.

Child-rearing provision 


If you took time off from work or worked less to look after your young children, you may be able to increase the amount of your CPP retirement pension and other CPP benefits by applying for the child-rearing provisions.

Tax information 


Tax slips (T4A(P), T4A(OAS) and NR4) lets you check and print T4s for the last 6 years if you received CPP and or OAS benefits. Tax slip mailing options lets you choose to receive your T4 electronically or by mail. You can later change the option and still check and print T4s.

View/Change my personal information 


In this section, you can check or modify your mailing address, telephone number and direct deposit information.

Payments 


In this section, you can view your CPP and OAS Payment information.

Contributions 


In this section, you can view your contributions, pensionable earnings, and view your estimated monthly CPP benefits. You can also request for your Statement of Contributions by mail.

Consent 


Use this application to name an authorized person (for example: your spouse, a member of your family, a friend or your accountant, etc.) to whom you give consent to communicate on your behalf with Service Canada about your CPP and OAS.
