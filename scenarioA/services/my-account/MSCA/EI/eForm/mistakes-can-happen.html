Mistakes can happen

We realize that mistakes can happen. If you make a mistake filling out forms or reports, or if there is a change in your circumstances that could affect your EI benefits, advise Service Canada immediately. Letting us know of a mistake or a change early is your best way to prevent future problems with your claim, or avoid a penalty or prosecution.
To make a voluntary disclosure, call our telephone information service at 1-800-206-7218, from 8:30 am to 4:30 pm, and press "0" to speak to a representative. You can also write or go to your Service Canada Centre.
